The fin gets closer, and closer, and you couldn't move an inch from where you were swimming from. So..is this goodbye? You close your eyes, hoping for...whatever that thing is to eat you up instantly so you wouldn't feel the pain, but then..

"woof woof!"

"The most dangerous dog in the world" by San Diego Shooter is licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 2.0 .

You open your eyes and see that it was just a swimming puppy following you the whole time! It appears that it has tricked you to grab your attention, and now it wants to play! It brought over a ball so you can play water fetch with it. You decided to play with the dog seeing as you had nothing else planned but to swim for a little more.
